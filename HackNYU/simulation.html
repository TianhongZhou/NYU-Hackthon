Global Warming Cycle

Click the arrows!

Emissions of polluting gases
will generate acid rain.

Industrial factories will emit
waste gases into water bodies.

Acid rain will cause land desertification,
which reduces the plants on Earth.

Water vaporized from contaminated
water bodies will enter the atmosphere,
further speeding up global warming.
