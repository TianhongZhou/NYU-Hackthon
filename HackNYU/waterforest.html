Water Contamination and Reduced Forest Resources

Water Contamination

The rising sea level is a considerable problem nowadays, since it can directly lead to the destruction of fresh water resources. Moreover, human activities produce large amount of toxic chemial wastes that are being emitted into the water bodies, including oil leakage and the destruction of marine ecological enviroment.

Reduced Forest Resources

As human gradually industrializing the world, more damages are caused to vegetation and plants due to excessive deforestation for timber and land reclamation. Such human activities reduce the ability of the nature to maintain its ecological environment, and could possibly cause desertification, further contributing to the increasing temperature of our world.
